About Kinnser

Kinnser Software Inc. was founded as a healthcare consulting agency specializing in clinical forms management and reporting. While working with home health care agencies it became clear a software solution could be used to manage the workflow and data of an agency in a way that benefited the agency more than traditional means. This premise laid the groundwork for our Agency Manager application.

Agency Manager is a software application tailored for home health agencies that not only manages clinician schedules, but also provides a robust platform for remote submission of all common forms required for Medicare home health patients. These forms include nursing progress notes, physical therapy progress notes, physical therapy assessments, OASIS forms, and supervision forms.
